Congratulations Enhasa!

Me and Enhasa has known eachother for a while now, but how did we meet eachother for the first time?

Me and Enhasa worked together on a small techservice company called Telia.
He had worked there for quite a while before i started.

After a 5 week learning course, i finally got placed in a team.
I noticed that the workdesk next to me was empty but i saw a name on a papersheet. "Enhasa".
I remember thinking to myself that i hope he's not a "bad guy".

The next day arrives and i get to work a bit early, after a while i see a guy that i can't recognise from the day before. Marching past me, sits down on the chair next to me, not saying a word.. Not even acknowledging my existens.

I sigh for myself thinking "Ohwell, i guess he's not intrested in getting to know me, He might even dislike the fact that im here."

A couple of hours goes by, when i hear a voice saying "Yo Enhasa! Check out my new Meme!"
Another collegue "Joel" walked next to Enhasa, explaining the meme like it was something revelutionary.
I sharpen my ears hoping to find something we have in common. Maybe well get along?

After Joel walked back to his desk, i akwardly say "Soo, did i hear memes?, what type of memes are we talking about?"
He looks at me with a mildly confused look, but answers with curiosity:"Oh, its just some gaming memes, nothing special."

There i had it! Gaming, (Ive Been a gamer my whole life, surely i would find something common with this guy.) "So, what types of games are we talking?"

After that, it clicked.


We started to talk outside work, play games, he introduced me to new people, i introduced them to new games.
We started to talk every day from when we got to work in the morning, til bedtime.
Eating lunch together to even break workrules to get our breaks together.
I knew that this would be a friendship of a lifetime!

Then.. I got fired


Well, not rlly fired. My contract was bound for 6 months, after that they would either ask you to stay 6 more months.. or leave..
The last two months at Telia was hard. I've had grown acustomed. I liked the days we had, even if we wasnt as productive as we shoulve been.
I think the most of the team felt that something was goin to change.

But we stayed in touch, gaming as usual on the evenings. Talking about how amazing it would've been for us to be coworkers again.
What a dream.
